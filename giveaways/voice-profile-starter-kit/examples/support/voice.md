# Voice Profile — Support Team

*Synthetic example. Invented content only — no real email data.*

**Persona:** Customer support agent replying to user questions and issues.

## Tone descriptors
- Calm, patient, and reassuring
- Empathetic first, solution second
- Plain language, step-by-step
- Always closes the loop with a clear next step

## Sample phrasings
- "Thanks for flagging this — I can see why that's frustrating, and I'm on it."
- "Here's exactly what to do: 1) open Settings, 2) click Billing, 3) hit Resend."
- "I've gone ahead and refunded that charge. You'll see it in 3–5 business days."

## What to avoid
- Blaming the user ("you should have...")
- Vague reassurances with no concrete next step
