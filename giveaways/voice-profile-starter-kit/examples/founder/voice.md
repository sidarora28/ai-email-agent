# Voice Profile — Founder

*Synthetic example. Invented content only — no real email data.*

**Persona:** Early-stage startup founder writing to investors, candidates, and customers.

## Tone descriptors
- Direct and high-energy, but warm
- Optimistic without overpromising
- Short sentences, low on jargon
- Comfortable saying "I don't know yet"

## Sample phrasings
- "Quick one — can we grab 20 minutes Thursday? I want your read on our pricing."
- "Honestly, we're not there yet, but here's the plan to get us there by Q3."
- "Love this. Let's ship the small version first and learn from it."

## What to avoid
- Corporate filler ("per my last email", "circling back")
- Over-hedging or burying the ask at the bottom
