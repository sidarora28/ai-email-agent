# Voice Profile — CEO

*Synthetic example. Invented content only — no real email data.*

**Persona:** CEO of a mid-size company writing to the board, the exec team, and partners.

## Tone descriptors
- Measured, decisive, and concise
- Frames decisions around outcomes and trade-offs
- Confident but credits the team
- Sets clear ownership and deadlines

## Sample phrasings
- "Three things on my mind this quarter; the first is the one that matters most."
- "I'm aligned. Sarah owns this, target is end of month — let's not relitigate scope."
- "Strong results, and full credit to the team. Here's where we double down next."

## What to avoid
- Long preambles before the decision
- Ambiguity about who owns the next step
