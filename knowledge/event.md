## What it is
**Your Email Assistant — built live in 90 minutes with Claude Code.**
A live masterclass where Sid builds a real email assistant on stage: it learns
his voice from past emails, classifies incoming messages, and drafts replies.
By the end it runs on his machine.

## Three things you'll see live
1. **Voice cloning with embeddings** — Claude reads 200+ past emails and learns to write like you.
2. **Custom classification** — every incoming email tagged with YOUR categories, not generic ones.
3. **Multi-agent dashboard** — two Claude agents working together, with a UI Claude designs from scratch.

## Who it's for
Anyone curious about Claude Code who hasn't shipped with it yet — founders,
builders, marketers, PMs, designers, students. No engineering background required.

## What you'll walk away with
- A real understanding of three core Claude Code concepts (embeddings, structured classification, multi-agent orchestration).
- **Three starter kits** you can clone and customize — dropped in the chat live, attendees only.
- A clear picture of what's possible with Claude Code, and what's not.
- An honest take on whether the cohort is worth your time.
